Privacy Policy

BNI Go Green Data Downloader is designed to automate the download of reports from BNI Connect for Go Green reporting purposes.

The extension requires users to provide their BNI Connect credentials to access their reports.

User credentials are used solely for authentication with BNI Connect.

The extension does not sell, share, transfer, or distribute user data to third parties.

The extension does not transmit credentials or report data to developer-controlled servers.

Data is used only to perform the functionality requested by the user.
